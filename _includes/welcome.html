About

The Hop Ice Cream Cafe has been proudly serving Asheville, NC since 1978 with premium quality homemade 16% butterfat Dairy Ice Cream, from-scratch Vegan Ice Cream and Sorbet, fair-trade/organic Coffee and Espresso, unique specialty desserts and more. In addition The Hop hosts a variety of Birthday Parties, Free Family Friendly Entertainment, and whimsical special events at each of its three Asheville locations. Ashley and Greg Garrison, owners and joy makers, began working at The Hop in 2003 as UNC Asheville students, then took the reins in 2008, and have since tirelessly worked to create a genuinely community-minded business around delicous hand-crafted Ice Cream.
